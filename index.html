About Dr. Srinivasan Kanduri

Dr. Srinivasan Kanduri is a renowned astrologer and spiritual guide with over 20+ years of dedicated practice in Vedic astrology and Vaasthu Shastra. His profound knowledge and accurate predictions have transformed the lives of thousands of individuals, guiding them towards harmony, prosperity, and spiritual fulfillment.

Dr. Srinivasan specializes in comprehensive horoscope analysis, life predictions, match-making, and Vaasthu consultations for homes, offices, and commercial establishments. His approach combines ancient Vedic wisdom with practical solutions tailored to modern life challenges.

With his compassionate guidance and deep understanding of planetary influences, Dr. Srinivasan has earned the trust and respect of clients across Andhra Pradesh and beyond. His consultations are known for their accuracy, clarity, and transformative impact on people's lives.
